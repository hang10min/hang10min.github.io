About Me

4月 14 2026

张百苓，字凌云，号云水禅心。1971年出生于河北省文安县。自幼受家庭熏陶，酷爱琴、棋、书、画。书法始学欧、颜、柳、赵等楷书字帖。后拜于书画大师王书明门下，钻研二王、苏、 黄、米、蔡、张旭等诸多书法大家的行草摹本。其作品崇尚法度、结体精严、端庄中正、温润雅致、极富流美之感，给人以气力相通、意气天成的感觉。作品多次参加国内书法大赛并获奖。 精于中国象棋，曾获新中国象棋平台特级棋手评定。自幼修习口琴，平素喜爱观察并描摹骏马的神态动作，尤爱画竹，笔下竹枝清雅挺拔，风骨尽显。


Zhang Bailing, English name Liam, courtesy name Lingyun (Beyond Clouds, with Lofty Ambition), art name Yunshui Chanxin (Serene Mind Like Clouds and Water).

Born in Wen’an County, Hebei Province in 1971, he was nurtured by a family steeped in culture from an early age, and developed a profound love for the four elegant arts: music, chess, calligraphy and painting.

He first studied regular script by tracing the models of Ouyang Xun, Yan Zhenqing, Liu Gongquan and Zhao Mengfu. Later, he devoted himself to the running-cursive styles of the Two Wangs, Su Shi, Huang Tingjian, Mi Fu, Cai Xiang, Zhang Xu and other great masters.

His works uphold rigorous rules of calligraphy, with refined structure, dignified balance, gentle grace and flowing rhythm, exuding a natural harmony of strength and spirit. Besides,His works have been widely exhibited in national calligraphy competitions and received numerous awards.

He is proficient in Chinese chess and has been awarded the title of Grandmaster on the New China Chess platform. He learned to play the harmonica at an early age and is fond of observing and painting the demeanor and movements of horses.He particularly enjoys painting bamboo; the bamboo in his works is elegant, upright and vigorous, fully revealing its noble character.

书法作品 calligraphy


静气 | 静心

作品以行书书写 “静心” 与 “静气” 各二字，笔力苍劲厚重，墨色浓淡有致。起笔果敢，收笔沉稳，结构开阔大气：“静心” 将 “静” 的舒展与 “心” 的内敛相融，“静气” 以 “静” 的沉敛衬 “气” 的贯通，两作气韵同源、意境相生。字里行间尽显从容心境，法度严谨又灵动自然，观之令人心平气和，恰合 “静心”“静气” 的修心之旨。

This pair of running-script calligraphy works, “Calm Mind” and “Serene Spirit”, features bold, robust brushstrokes and well-modulated ink. With decisive starts and steady finishes, the characters are grand and balanced: “Calm Mind” blends the expansiveness of “Calm” with the restraint of “Mind”, while “Serene Spirit” pairs the stillness of “Serene” with the flow of “Spirit”. Both works exude a tranquil, composed state of mind, balancing rigorous calligraphic discipline with natural fluidity, perfectly embodying the essence of inner peace.

攀高贵在少年时

作品以行书写就 “攀高贵在少年时”，笔力遒劲温润，结体端庄舒展，墨色浓淡相宜。整幅字气韵贯通，将父亲对两个儿子的殷切期许融于笔墨：既以 “攀高” 寄寓志存高远的期许，又以 “贵在少年时” 劝勉珍惜韶华、勤勉奋进。

Great Heights Await the Young

Written in running script, the calligraphy work “True Nobility Lies in One’s Youth” features vigorous yet gentle brushstrokes, balanced and graceful character structures, and well-modulated ink tones. The entire piece flows with a cohesive spirit, weaving a father’s earnest expectations for his two sons into every stroke: “Reaching great heights” embodies his wish for them to aim high, while “cherishing one’s youth” urges them to value their prime years and strive diligently.
