About Me

4月 14 2026

张百苓，字凌云，号云水禅心。1971年出生于河北省文安县。自幼受家庭熏陶，酷爱琴、棋、书、画。书法始学欧、颜、柳、赵等楷书字帖。后拜于书画大师王书明门下，钻研二王、苏、 黄、米、蔡、张旭等诸多书法大家的行草摹本。其作品崇尚法度、结体精严、端庄中正、温润雅致、极富流美之感，给人以气力相通、意气天成的感觉。作品多次参加国内书法大赛并获奖。 精于中国象棋，曾获新中国象棋平台特级棋手评定。自幼修习口琴，平素喜爱观察并描摹骏马的神态动作，尤爱画竹，笔下竹枝清雅挺拔，风骨尽显。


Zhang Bailing, English name Liam, courtesy name Lingyun (Beyond Clouds, with Lofty Ambition), art name Yunshui Chanxin (Serene Mind Like Clouds and Water).

Born in Wen’an County, Hebei Province in 1971, he was nurtured by a family steeped in culture from an early age, and developed a profound love for the four elegant arts: music, chess, calligraphy and painting.

His works uphold rigorous rules of calligraphy, with refined structure, dignified balance, gentle grace and flowing rhythm, exuding a natural harmony of strength and spirit. Besides,His works have been exhibited in some competitions and received some awards.

He is an accomplished player of Chinese chess, holding the Grandmaster title on the Xin Qiyi platform. He learned to play the harmonica at a young age and loves painting horses. He also has a special fondness for painting bamboo.

书法作品

静气 | 静心

作品以行书书写 “静心” 与 “静气” 各二字，笔力苍劲厚重，结构开阔大气：“静心” 将 “静” 的舒展与 “心” 的内敛相融，“静气” 以 “静” 的沉敛衬 “气” 的贯通，两作气韵同源、意境相生。观之令人心平气和，恰合 “静心”“静气” 的修心之旨。

These two running-script works, Calm Mind and Serene Spirit, feature powerful brushstrokes and harmonious composition. Calm and graceful, they embody peace and self-cultivation.

Knowledge Knows No Bounds

极拳道

The Tao of Tai Chi

诚信辉煌
行草笔力雄健，结体开张大气，墨色浓润饱满，起收顿挫分明，笔势贯通一气。四字布局错落有致，将 “诚信” 的厚重与 “辉煌” 的昂扬融于笔墨，尽显刚劲风骨与进取气象。

Integrity & Glory
Bold running-cursive script with powerful strokes and full ink, conveying integrity and ambition in a dynamic, majestic composition.

精气神
行草笔势连绵飞动，墨色干湿相生，线条苍劲灵动。三字结体奇崛洒脱，将 “精、气、神” 的生命境界融于笔墨，气韵贯通，神采飞扬。

Vitality, Energy & Spirit
Flowing cursive script with dynamic strokes, blending vigor, energy and spirit in a vivid, expressive calligraphic work.


攀高贵在少年时

这幅行书作品以 “攀高贵在少年时” 为内容，笔力温润遒劲，结体端庄舒展，将父亲对儿子志存高远、惜时奋进的殷切期许融于笔墨，气韵贯通，意蕴深远。
This running-script calligraphy, “Great Heights Await the Young”, features steady, elegant brushstrokes. It conveys a father’s earnest wish for his sons to aim high, cherish youth, and strive diligently.
